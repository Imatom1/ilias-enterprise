How It Started

Built Out of Frustration.
Fueled by a Better Idea.

My name is Ilias, and I started this company with one conviction: you shouldn't need a big budget or a corporate connection to get great work done.

I've seen too many people get overcharged for mediocre software, locked out of proper tech support, or priced out of real tutoring. The markup wasn't justified by the quality — it was justified by the middlemen. Agencies, account managers, third-party platforms. Everyone taking a cut before the actual work even began.

"Every project I take on is scoped honestly, priced fairly, and delivered personally. No handoffs. No surprises."

Ili Enterprises is my answer to that. Three divisions — Ilitech, Iliteach, and Ilitrade — built from the ground up to give individuals, students, and small businesses access to the same quality that used to be reserved for people with much deeper pockets.

Whether you need a custom app, a tutoring session, or a platform to sell without fees, the standard is the same: direct work from someone who cares about the outcome as much as you do.
