Who We Are

Built by Ilias.
Built for You.

My name is Ilias, and I started this company with one conviction: you shouldn't need a big budget or a corporate connection to get great work done. I've seen too many people get overcharged for mediocre software, locked out of proper tech support, or priced out of real tutoring.

Ilias Enterprises is my answer to that. Three divisions — Ilitech, Iliteach, and Ilitrade — built from the ground up to give individuals, students, and small businesses access to the same quality that used to be reserved for people with much deeper pockets.

Every project I take on is scoped honestly, priced fairly, and delivered personally. No account managers, no handoffs, no surprises. Just direct work from someone who cares about the outcome as much as you do.
