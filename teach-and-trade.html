The Two Divisions

What Each One Does

Iliteach
Education · Tutoring · Resources

Iliteach is the education arm of Ilias Enterprises. The goal is simple: give students and self-learners access to real academic support without the price tag of a tutoring center or the noise of a crowded platform.

Whether you need a focused cheat sheet for an upcoming exam, a one-on-one tutoring session that actually moves at your pace, or access to a library of genuine student work to reference and learn from — Iliteach is being built to cover all of it.

In Development
Ilitrade
Marketplace · Resellers · Advertising

Ilitrade is the commerce division — a marketplace built specifically for people who are tired of platform fees eating into their margins. No percentage cuts, no listing taxes, no algorithm-gated visibility.

The platform is being designed for resellers who need fair listing tools, service providers who want to advertise without inflated rates, and buyers who want a cleaner, more direct experience from real sellers.
